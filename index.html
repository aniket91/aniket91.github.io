Hi there, I'm Aniket!

As a wise man once said -

If you have an apple and I have an apple and we exchange apples then you and I will still each have one apple. But if you have an idea and I have an idea and we exchange these ideas, then each of us will have two ideas.

Unlike money knowledge increases by sharing. So speak up, talk, ask whatever it takes to quench your thirst for knowledge. Have a doubt? Ask. Have an idea? Propose. Have a through? Discus. It's all out there, up for grabs. All it takes is initiative and willingness to know more. Do not judge your day by how much money you have earned, it all about how much new you have learned.


All the best!
